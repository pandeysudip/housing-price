US-Housing

Housing market in different cities in the US fluctuates a lot. Housing price is not always related to a single factor. In this project, we uncovered patterns on housing prices in different US cities. We looked on how the city population, income, poverty, and different other factors that we get from census API like education, transportation affects the housing price.To predict the house value, we built different supervised linear models from scikit learn. Our best model is Random Forest Regressor which has a mean absolute error of around $27000.
