This website presents an overview of the US agriculture over the year 2017. You will find various information relating to every type of crop, such as news articles, representative images, sale values, weather, or even graphics. The combination of these visualization tools may help the user get a better understanding of the might of the US agricultural productivity.

Some quick numbers:
Total agricultural sales in 2017 amounted to $314 billion, divided between three types of products: Field crops ($250 Billion), Fruits ($47 Billion), and Vegetables ($16 Billion).
